Welcome to the website of the Economie expérimentale et comportementale-TSE!

This group brings together TSE researchers and graduate students working on Behavioral Economics, Experimental Economics, and at the interface between Economics and other Sciences, notably Psychology, Sociology, Neuroscience, Biology and Political Science. You can watch a quick example of our research work:

Note that since the creation of the IAST (www.iast.fr), many of the activities that were formerly organized by the BEE group are now merged with activities at the IAST.

Group activities:

  • IAST seminars from various disciplines
  • IAST biology-economics seminar series
  • IAST political science seminar series

We are also organizing summer/winter schools, workshops, conferences and reading groups on various topics. For more information see activities announced at IAST.
